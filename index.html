Areas of Expertise

Specialized
Urologic Care

Dr. Sanchez combines advanced robotic surgical techniques with personalized, compassionate care — guiding every patient through their journey with expertise and dedication.

01

Robotic Surgery

Minimally invasive da Vinci robotic procedures for precise resection, reduced recovery time, and superior oncologic outcomes for bladder, kidney, prostate, and adrenal cancers.

02

Urologic Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care including prostate, kidney, bladder, testicular, and adrenal tumors — from diagnosis through multimodal treatment planning and survivorship.

03

Kidney Cancer

Nephron-sparing surgery, radical nephrectomy, and ablative therapies for renal tumors — with a focus on preserving kidney function whenever possible.

04

Prostate Cancer

Active surveillance counseling, robotic-assisted radical prostatectomy, and coordination of radiation or systemic therapies tailored to each patient's disease stage and values.

05

Bladder Cancer

TURBT, cystoscopy-based surveillance, intravesical therapies, and robotic cystectomy with urinary diversion for invasive disease.

06

General Urology

Management of benign prostatic hyperplasia, kidney stones, urinary incontinence, hematuria, recurrent UTIs, and male reproductive health.

The Process

A Partnership
in Your Care

From first consultation to long-term follow-up, every step is guided by precision, transparency, and genuine partnership.

01

Comprehensive Consultation

Every new patient visit begins with a thorough review of your history, imaging, and prior workup. Dr. Sanchez listens carefully and ensures you have time to share your concerns and ask questions.

02

Precise Diagnosis & Staging

Using the latest imaging modalities, pathology correlation, and genomic tools where appropriate, Dr. Sanchez establishes an accurate diagnosis and disease stage to guide treatment decisions.

03

Personalized Treatment Planning

No two patients are the same. Dr. Sanchez presents all appropriate options — surgical, non-surgical, and surveillance — helping you understand tradeoffs so you can make an informed, values-aligned decision.

04

Minimally Invasive Surgery

When surgery is indicated, Dr. Sanchez leverages robotic and laparoscopic techniques to minimize incisions, reduce blood loss, shorten hospital stays, and accelerate your return to normal life.

05

Ongoing Follow-Up & Survivorship

Dr. Sanchez remains a partner long after treatment. Structured surveillance programs and open communication ensure any concerns are addressed promptly and your long-term health is optimized.
