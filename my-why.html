My Why

Dr. Desiree Sanchez is a fellowship-trained urologist and robotic urologic oncologist dedicated to the highest standard of surgical care. Her training spans the most rigorous programs in urology, equipping her with advanced skills in robotic and minimally invasive surgery.

Dr. Sanchez approaches each patient as a partner — taking time to understand their concerns, explain all options clearly, and build a treatment plan that reflects both the best evidence-based medicine and the patient's individual goals.

Her path to medicine was shaped by a deep conviction that patients deserve a physician who listens first and operates with precision — treating the whole person, not just the diagnosis. That belief drives every consultation, every surgery, every follow-up.
